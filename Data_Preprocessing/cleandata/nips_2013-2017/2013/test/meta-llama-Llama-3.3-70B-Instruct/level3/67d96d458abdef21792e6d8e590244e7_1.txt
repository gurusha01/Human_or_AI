This paper presents a novel approach to visual concept learning, a task that involves learning a visual concept from a small number of positive examples. The authors develop a Bayesian framework that combines probabilistic predictions from visual classifiers with a Bayesian generalization model to determine the appropriate level of abstraction for a given concept.
