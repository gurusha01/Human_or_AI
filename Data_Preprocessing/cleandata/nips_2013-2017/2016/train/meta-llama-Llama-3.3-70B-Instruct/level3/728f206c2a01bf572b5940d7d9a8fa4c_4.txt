This paper presents a novel approach to modeling and analyzing crowdsourcing systems using an information-theoretic framework. The paper provides theoretical proof, quantitative comparisons, and applications in data completion and denoising to demonstrate the usefulness of the new objective function.

The authors provide a comprehensive overview of the related work, including the Dawid and Skene algorithm, and other recent research in the field.
