This paper addresses safe adaptive importance sampling, a topic currently garnering significant interest within the community. Notable aspects of this work include the method's generality, allowing integration with numerous existing algorithms, as well as the inclusion of both theoretical analyses and practical experiments to substantiate the proposed approach.  
 
However, one concern that needs to be addressed is the presentation of the figures in Section 5, which are challenging to interpret due to their small size. These figures, along with their labels, should be enlarged to enhance readability for the audience.
