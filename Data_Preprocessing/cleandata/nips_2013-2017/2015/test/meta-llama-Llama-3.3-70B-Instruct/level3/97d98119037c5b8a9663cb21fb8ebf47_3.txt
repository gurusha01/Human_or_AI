This paper proposes a new algorithm, PEGI, for Independent Component Analysis (ICA) in the presence of Gaussian noise. The algorithm is based on a pseudo-Euclidean gradient iteration and is shown to be efficient and accurate in recovering the mixing matrix A. The paper also provides a theoretical analysis of the algorithm and demonstrates its performance on simulated data.
