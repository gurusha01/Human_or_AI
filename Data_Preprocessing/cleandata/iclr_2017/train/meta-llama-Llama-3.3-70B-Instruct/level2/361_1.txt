This paper presents a novel approach to modeling learning curves of iterative machine learning methods using Bayesian neural networks. The authors propose a specialized neural network architecture with a learning curve layer that improves learning curve predictions. The paper claims to contribute to the field by studying how well Bayesian neural networks can fit learning curves, developing a new basis function layer for learning curve prediction, and evaluating the predictive quality of the model for both completely new learning curves and extrapolating partially-observed curves.
