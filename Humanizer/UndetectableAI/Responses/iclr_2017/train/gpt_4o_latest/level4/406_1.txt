This study examines improving ensembles in policy gradient training methods which have been overlooked but show potential in this domain for a time; finally delving into this intriguing subject matter here addresses the gap, in research effectively and clearly outlines it for readers to comprehend easily.Section 4 raises questions that are articulated skillfully and stimulate critical thinking effectively. 
Nevertheless​ the paper has some drawbacks; especially in that all findings are limited to a highly specific range of areas and settings​. I look forward to a revised version, for a journal where these tests are expanded to include a variety of sources/targets/domains and parameters​.
