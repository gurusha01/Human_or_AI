This research paper delves into the use of optimization in policy gradient training—an area that has not received enough attention until now.The authors’ examination of this idea is a contribution to the field.The paper is well written. Presents its content clearly for easy comprehension.Section 4 poses. Relevant questions, for further consideration. 
However there are some shortcomings in the study as well. Its results are limited to an area and set of conditions. To improve the study it would be helpful to replicate the experiments, in source domains target domains and parameter combinations. This issue is expected to be addressed in the journal publication.
