The paper showcases advantages; particularly its extension of readability score usage, to the Vietnamese language has led to a thoroughly validated score.  
The tool has some drawbacks well; especially in its applicability to the ACL domain where readability metrics hold less importance and are better suited for educational and public communication purposes instead.In addition to this there are a minor grammar mistakes in the writing. Another point of concern is the evaluation of PDS 2 and PDV2 attributes from studies leaving uncertainty, about how the former readability assessments compare practically with the new measure. 
It would be more impactful if the dataset and its corresponding labels were shared openly for access and use, in the research paper to expand its relevance beyond just basic readability evaluations to exploring more sophisticated feature selection techniques.
